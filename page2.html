How Much Does This Job Pay?

As of August of 2018, the average base pay for a computer hardware engineer was around $118,755 per year.

According to the Bereau of Labor Statistics, in May of 2017, the average hourly wage was $57.52, and the average annual wage was $199,650. The median hourly pay was $55.35, and the median annual pay was $115,120.
