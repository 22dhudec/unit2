Computer Hardware Engineering

What is a Computer Hardware Engineer?

A computer hardware engineer is someone who is in charge of the hardware and inner workings of a computer. They work to improve the function, design, and layout of a computer by designing and constructing the computer's main components. They are expected to make the computer run faster and more efficiently. They develop and run rigorous tests on components, such as computer chips and motherboards.
